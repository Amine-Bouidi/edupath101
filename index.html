Frequently asked questions
Can I upgrade my plan later?
Yes — you only pay the difference between your current plan and the new one.
How do I choose my modules?
After purchasing, you unlock any modules of your choice from any semester and university.
When does the tutoring session happen?
After purchasing the Premium plan, use the "Book tutoring" form to schedule your session.
Is access time-limited?
No — once you purchase a plan, you keep access to those modules permanently.
